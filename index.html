The Bosnian Buffet is an establishment created by Bosnian immigrants in Chicago who aim to show the world the beautiful side of their country.

Being under Ottoman rule for more than 400 years, Bosnian cuisine is heavily influenced by Ottoman cuisine. Our restaurant gives you the chance to eat what the Sultans ate!
